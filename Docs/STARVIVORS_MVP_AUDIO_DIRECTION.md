# STARVIVORS MVP AUDIO DIRECTION

This file defines the audio direction for STARVIVORS.

Audio is not required in the first documentation-only task and should not be implemented until a future prompt asks for it.

## Audio Goal

STARVIVORS audio should support arcade sci-fi readability and strong game feel.

The sound should help the player understand:

- Weapon firing
- Impacts
- Enemy destruction
- Scrap collection
- Black hole danger
- Low hull or low shield warnings
- Upgrade availability
- Upgrade and shop confirmations

## MVP Sound Direction

Recommended direction:

- Arcade sci-fi sound effects
- Clear weapon sounds
- Strong impact feedback
- Distinct pickup sounds
- Black hole ambience or pull tension
- Warning sounds for critical danger
- Synth or electronic music direction, to be decided later

## Implementation Rule

Do not add audio systems, assets, packages, or playback code until a future task explicitly requests audio.

When audio is implemented later, it should be modular and easy to disable for testing.

## Priority Order For Future Audio

When audio work begins, prioritize:

1. Player weapon fire
2. Enemy and debris impact feedback
3. Player damage feedback
4. Scrap pickup feedback
5. Upgrade available and upgrade selected feedback
6. Black hole danger ambience
7. Music
